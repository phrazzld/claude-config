## Best Practices

- Keep single posts under 280 chars
- Threads work well for launches
- Visual content (screenshot, demo GIF) increases engagement
- Reply to every comment in first hour
- Don't overdo hashtags (2-4 max)
- Morning EST or lunchtime PST perform well
